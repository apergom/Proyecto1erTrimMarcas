About me & this project

Sup. If you're reading this you really went deep into this page just to read about me.

Lemme get started. I'm Manu (Antonio Manuel Pérez Gómez professionally, and MdeManola in many other places). I'm just a guy who's passionate about videogames and music (especially when it comes to soundtracks and game OSTs).

Now, just so you know, this wasn't my first idea when making this project. I wanted to make some kind of tracker to "track" (yeah, I just did that) your progress in videogames just like a digital checklist, but after some thought, I had the brightest idea ever: make my own version of one of my favorite games' Wikipedia. And that's exactly what you're into right now.

I took inspiration from other well-made sites such as GGDeals or the REAL ULTRAKILL Wiki.

Oh, and before you get lost among the important people that worked on this masterpiece (the game): I have no idea why I'm doing this in English.

About Hakita

Hakita is the pseudonym of Arsi Patala, the creator, main developer, and composer of the video game ULTRAKILL. He is known for his work on this fast-paced, ultraviolent old school first-person shooter.

About New Blood Interactive

New Blood Interactive is a video game publisher and developer founded in 2014 by Aaron Alexander, Kreyg DeZago, and Dave Oshry. Initially focused on virtual reality games, the company shifted towards retro-inspired shooters like ULTRAKILL, DUSK, and others.
