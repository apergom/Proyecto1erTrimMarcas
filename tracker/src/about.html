About me & this project

Sup. If you are reading this you really went deep into this page just to read about me.

Lemme get started.

I'm Manu (Antonio Manuel Pérez Gómez just professionally and MdeManola in many other places). I'm just a guy who's passionate about videogames and music (imagine when it comes to music IN videogames).

Now, just so you know, this wasn't my first idea when making this proyect. I wanted to make some kind of tracker to "track" (yeah, I just did that) your progress in videogames just like your own Steam page can do but in some sort of external web like Last.fm for Spotify. I had to change it due to some "functionality" problems and lots of things that I didn't know how to implement in there.

After that, I had the brightest idea I could've ever had. Make my own version of one of my favorite games Wikipedia, and that's exactly what you are into right now. I first thought about Cuphead, since it's my favorite game, but that would've been the easy way out. I went down my Steam Library and replayed ULTRAKILL (another one of my favorites) and started working in this "thing".

I took inspiration of some other well done webs such as GGDeals or the REAL ULTRAKILL wiki.

Oh, and before you get lost into the important people that worked in this masterpiece (the game). No tengo ni idea de porque lo he hecho en inglés.

About Hakita

Hakita is the pseudonym of Arsi Patala, the creator, main developer, and composer of the video game ULTRAKILL. He is known for his work on this fast-paced, ultraviolent old school first-person shooter game with character action influences. Hakita also shares music, movies, books, and games that he likes, and streams gameplay, including ULTRAKILL and other titles. He is a notable figure in the gaming community, particularly around ULTRAKILL's fan base, and is based in Finland.

About New Blood Interactive

New Blood Interactive is a video game publisher and developer founded in 2014 by Aaron Alexander, Kreyg DeZago, and Dave Oshry. Initially focused on virtual reality games, the company shifted towards publishing and developing retro-style shooter games, gaining success with titles like Dusk, Amid Evil, Faith: The Unholy Trinity, and Gloomwood. They operate both as a publisher and in-house developer, producing games known for their classic shooter feel and aesthetics. Recently, they announced plans to port their games to Xbox Series X/S and optimize them for the ROG Xbox Ally platform. Their portfolio includes notable games such as Ultrakill (2020) and the upcoming Tenebris Somnia (2025).
